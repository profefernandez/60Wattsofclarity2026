# Organizational AI Policy Checklist

- Define the purpose of AI use.
- Identify approved tools and approved data.
- Decide what data must never be entered into an AI system.
- Review privacy, retention, and consent requirements.
- Assign a human reviewer for important outputs.
- Set a process for bias and error review.
- Write a staff training plan.
- Document vendor review and security questions.
- Add an escalation path for incidents or concerns.
- Review the policy on a set schedule.
