# Case Study Summary

## Project
Automating intake screening for a regional family services agency.

## Challenge
The agency had a growing backlog of intake paperwork and a delay between first contact and assignment.

## Approach
- Reviewed the intake workflow.
- Built an AI-assisted screening step to sort referrals by urgency.
- Kept staff in control of the final decision.

## Outcome
- Intake processing time dropped by 68%.
- Average wait time moved from 14 days to 4 days.
- Staff spent more time with higher-need cases.

## What this shows
AI works best when it reduces manual work, supports staff judgment, and fits the existing process.
